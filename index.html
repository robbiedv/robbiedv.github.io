About

hi, my name's robbie, some people call me rob. i'm a young developer and designer looking to debut into the world of tech, at least officially. i've been dabbling with html and css from the day i realized i could use it to customize my high school blog. since then i've built a couple of sites for friends and family. i always thought of my web-development knack as a hobby, never a career, until i saw an ad for the 'grow with google' scholarship program. since i had nothing to lose, i applied. i havn't looked back since then, earning a spot in

the 'challenge course' and then being awarded a scholarship by google to udacity's web-development program. i'm currently using this collective knowledge to build beautiful, functional and intuitive web pages and apps. about me personally, i'm married to the love of my life, emma, and we just recently had our first kid, thomas. springfield, il is home, where we all live in a cozy apartment with hardwood floors that squeak to raise the dead.
